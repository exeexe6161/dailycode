Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-25

Controller

The party named in the imprint is responsible for the data processing on this site. You will find the full details and the contact address in the imprint.

Local storage in your browser

This game stores data only locally in your browser using localStorage. It stores the current daily game, your progress, your statistics including the streak, and your language choice. This data stays on your device. There is no transfer to us or to anyone else. We run no server of our own for this data, use no tracking, perform no analysis, and use no cookies for advertising or tracking. The game embeds no services from other providers.

Legal basis

Local storage is technically necessary so the game works, keeps your progress, and maintains your statistics. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in functionality) together with Section 25(2) no. 2 TDDDG (formerly TTDSG), because the storage is strictly necessary for the function you explicitly requested. No consent banner is required for this.

Deletion

You can remove this locally stored data at any time by clearing the browser data for this site in your browser settings.

Hosting

When you open the site, the hosting provider processes technically necessary connection data, including the IP of the requesting device, in server logs. This is needed to deliver the site and to keep it secure and stable. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. The provider is Vercel Inc. (440 N Barranca Ave #4133, Covina, CA 91723, USA). This involves a transfer to a third country. It is safeguarded by standard contractual clauses.

Contact about privacy

For questions about data protection you can reach us at the contact address given in the imprint g.uer@aol.com.

Fonts

Fonts are served locally from our own server. There is no connection to third party providers such as Google Fonts, and no transfer to third parties takes place.
